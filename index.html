Healthy food – We are committed to sustainable and healthy gastronomy.

Our chickens grow, live and feed on the ground. They have the animal welfare certificate and are free of antibiotics, hormones and have not been genetically modified. Certified BIO, Halal and Groovy Chickens.

Raw material of km. 0 – Food from the garden directly to your body.

All the raw material we use in our restaurant is km. 0. We try to source all food from producers and farms in the province or nearby, so that it is as fresh as possible and has the least impact on the environment.
